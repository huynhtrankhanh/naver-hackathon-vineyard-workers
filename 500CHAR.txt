SmartMoney is an AI-powered personal finance app helping young people track expenses and achieve savings goals effortlessly. Built by Team Vineyard Workers for Naver Hackathon using React/Ionic, Express, and MongoDB. Features: multiple input methods (manual, receipt OCR, voice STT), Naver Clova AI expense categorization and personalized savings plans, budget management, goal tracking, and secure authentication. Transforms finance management into a simple, motivating experience.
